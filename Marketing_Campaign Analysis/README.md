## Objective

Analyze marketing funnel data to identify:

- Conversion drop-offs
- Channel performance
- Campaign performance
- Opportunities to improve lead-to-customer conversion

## Key Insights

- Google Ads generated the highest revenue.
- UG Admission campaign achieved the highest performance.
- Pan India produced the highest revenue.
- YouTube generated the lowest revenue.
- The largest audience segment was 17–21 years.
- Significant conversion drop-offs occurred between impressions and enrollments.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase investment in Google Ads.
- Improve lead nurturing to increase enrollments.
- Optimize YouTube campaigns.
- Expand high-performing campaigns.
- Focus on high-converting audience segments.
